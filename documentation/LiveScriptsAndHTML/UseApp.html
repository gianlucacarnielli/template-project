How To Use The App

  1. Click on the project shortcut "Run App".
  2. In the app, click on "Load Data..." and select an Excel spreadsheet in the "data" folder of the project.
  1. In the app, click on "Fit Data" to identify the best fit to the loaded data. A table with the fit coefficients will automatically display underneath the plot.
  2. Optionally, export the fit results to a MAT file in the desired folder.
